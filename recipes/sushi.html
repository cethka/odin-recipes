A Portion of the Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C).
  2. Bring water to a boil in a medium pot; stir in rice.
  3. Mix rice vinegar, sugar, and salt in a small bowl.
  4. Heat nori in the preheated oven until warm.
  5. Center 1 nori sheet on a bamboo sushi mat. Use wet hands to spread a thin layer of rice on top.
  6. Lift one end of the mat and roll it tightly over filling to make a complete roll.
  7. Use a wet, sharp knife to cut each roll into 4 to 6 slices.
